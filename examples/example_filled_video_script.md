# Video Script: A Grade 5 Student's Big Dream

**Content type:** video_testimonial
**Target platform:** instagram
**Target date:** 2026-07-10

## Hook (first 3 seconds)
"I want to be a pilot one day."

## Setup
Introduce Aisha M. (Grade 5) and the moment we're capturing: her first time
using the school's new flight-simulator STEM kit.

## Core moment / quote
> "At first I thought it would be too hard, but Ms. Grace showed me how the
> controls work and now I can land the plane by myself."

## Closing line
Every big dream starts with someone believing you can do it. What's yours?

## Notes for the filmer
Keep it under 45 seconds. Film in the STEM lab, natural light near the
window. Confirm signed consent form is on file before publishing (see
privacy-and-compliance skill).
